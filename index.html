I found crypto in 2016, years before it was a career. I was a Project Manager in edtech at Byju's, Toppr, and Vedantu, while spending nights reading whitepapers and tracking protocol launches. By 2021 I made the full jump and left a stable PM career to go all-in on Web3 marketing.

"I don't just run campaigns. I build the intelligence systems that tell me which campaigns to run."

Since then I've worked across the full ecosystem. At Autonity I launched the Forecastathon, a global prediction market trading competition that onboarded 2,500+ quant traders across 4 seasons. At RFX Exchange I drove 70K+ signups for a zkSync perp DEX alpha. At Relative Finance: 18K+ community. At Mozaic Finance: led a team of four.

More recently I've been building AI-powered tools to automate the intelligence work: competitor monitoring, narrative opportunity detection, and positioning analysis. Six shipped. This is what I do when I'm not doing marketing. I build things that make marketing smarter.
