I came into crypto in 2016 as a trader, not as a marketer looking for a niche. That matters because I learned markets from the inside first. I was still a project manager in edtech at Byju's, Toppr, and Vedantu when I started spending nights reading whitepapers, tracking launches, and figuring out what actually makes people adopt a protocol.

"Most Web3 marketing problems are adoption problems in disguise."

By 2021 I made the full jump. Since then I've worked across L1 infrastructure, perp DEXs, prediction markets, and DeFi. At Autonity I helped shape the mainnet narrative and ran Forecastathon across four seasons with 2,500+ traders and a 300,000 NTN prize pool. At RFX Exchange I drove 70K+ closed alpha signups on zkSync and built a 30+ KOL program from scratch. At Relative Finance I grew the community to 18K+. At Mozaic Finance I led a four-person marketing team.

The differentiator now is not that I use AI. Everyone says that. It is that I build the tools myself in Python and wire them into how I work: competitor monitoring, positioning engines, narrative alerts, and content systems that replace hours of manual research every week.
